Who is this person, and why should you care?

I began Uni education at the "right" age, but sucked at exams, so now I write in Rust, Python, and Go and only vibe code Somtimes^tm. However, since I'm in a NOC, I don't get a lot of time to do useful things for society like learn to program, maybe learn some Ethical Hacking to the extent I woudl like to. What I do get time for though, is working on my Git. Case and point, this portfolio. This port will slowly fill up, and with it, I hope whoever is reading this is able to grow along with me. Even if we can't all be Suma Cum Laude and or live in a country with free education and job programmes.
